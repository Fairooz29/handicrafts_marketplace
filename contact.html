Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

+

Browse our collection, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can pay using various methods including credit cards, bKash, and Nagad.

What is your shipping policy?

+

We offer standard shipping (3-5 business days) and express shipping (1-2 business days) within Bangladesh. International shipping is also available.

Can I return or exchange items?

+

Yes, we accept returns within 14 days of delivery for items in original condition. Custom or personalized items may have different return policies.

How do I become an artisan partner?

+

If you're a skilled artisan interested in partnering with us, please contact us through the form above or email us at artisans@craftsofbengal.com.
